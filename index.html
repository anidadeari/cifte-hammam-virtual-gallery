About this Museum

This virtual museum is a Computer Graphics project inspired by the atmosphere of Çifte Hammam in Skopje—historic stone spaces, soft light, and quiet movement through art.

The experience focuses on realism and mood: physically-based materials, dynamic spotlights, and interactive artwork information that appears as you explore the gallery.

Controls: W/A/S/D to move, Mouse to look around, Esc to release the cursor, and M to reopen the menu. Use G/P to start/stop the audio guide.
